SPLITWISE LLD DESIGN

I. IDENTIFYING REQUIREMENTS:

- Onboarding users into a common group (adding friends, creating room (CRUD))
- Users scan QR code to get bill, or enter the bill manually
- Splitting bill among users (unequal parts, percentge, parts/shares)
- User pays through a payment service on behalf of a group
- The remaining debt for each other user to be displayed in the group
- Other users should be able to pay up their remaining debts to the person they are indebted to.
- Users can track individual expenses as well.

II. IDENTIFYING ENTITIES:

- User class: userName, userAccountNumber, userBalance, userFriends
- Group class: groupName, groupDescription, groupAdmin, groupTotalExpense, groupMembers
- Expense class: userName, expenseName, totalExpenseAmount, previousTransactionsList, totalDebtAmount, listOfDebts
- Bill class: billID, billDescription, purchaseList, billAmount
- Debt class: debtID, lender(User), debtAmount, timeElapsed
- Transaction class: transactionID, sender, reciever, transactionAmount, transactionDateTime
